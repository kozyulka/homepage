About
Born in San Francisco (June 6, 1944), I was raised in Virginia. Discovered photography at the age of 11.  Now I am based in North Carolina and New York City and have been a full member of the Magnum Photos agency for over 20 years. I have also photographed extensively for National Geographic magazine. I addition, I am a publisher and editor of Burn Magazine, an online publication featuring the work of emerging photographers.
